pasta

Description

Creamy Tomato Rigatoni

Ingredients

  • butter
  • yellow onion
  • canned tomatoes
  • rigatoni pasta
  • heavy cream
  • salt & pepper
  • crushed red pepper
  • parmesan cheese
  • parsley

Steps

  1. cook chopped onion in butter
  2. add the tomatoes to the onion and simmer
  3. cook and drain the pasta
  4. stir cream, salt, black pepper, and crushed red pepper into tomato sauce
  5. add cooked pasta, and toss to coat
  6. add pasta water until sauce is desired consistency
  7. plate the pasta and sprinkle with parmesan
  8. garnish with parsley
  9. eat pasta
